About Me

Namaste! I am Mahabali, not the legendary king from Hindu mythology, but a modern-day artist with a passion for transforming stories, culture, and mythology into vibrant and enduring tattoos. With over 8 years of experience in the world of tattoo artistry, I've had the privilege of creating masterpieces on the canvas of skin, with a client base that spans across continents.
